Decent Health Care

At NTeli, we employ a combination of skills, apps, kits, and proven methodologies to get the best results in the shortest amount of time.

Overview

To implement the Salesforce healthcare cloud for the unique needs of the business

Decent, a healthcare insurance startup, offers affordable health insurance options by aligning incentives to improve health, reduce costs, and put members at the center of their care. Their target of Single Owner Business, has allowed them to focus on the unique needs of this segment and organically build a customer base around the Austin market.
